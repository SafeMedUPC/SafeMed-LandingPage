About Us

SafeMed

SafeMed is an innovative startup founded by Software Engineering students from the Faculty of Engineering at the Peruvian University of Applied Sciences. Our company focuses on developing IoT technology solutions, called MedSystem, for continuous monitoring of patients' vital signs. MedSystem is designed to meet the needs of patients with cardiac conditions and their treating physicians, enabling early detection of emergencies.

Patients

MedSystem is an intuitive and accessible platform that gives you the peace of mind of monitoring your heart rate in real-time. With our application connected to IoT sensors, you can visualize your vital signs, receive automatic alerts for anomalies, and ensure that your family members and doctors are immediately notified in case of emergency, all in one place, so you can focus on your well-being without complications.

Doctors / Specialists

MedSystem is your ally in efficiently and safely managing patients with cardiac conditions. With our platform, you can monitor your patients' heart rate in real-time, receive automatic alerts for irregular patterns, and quickly coordinate emergency care when necessary. Simplify your daily work and provide quality preventive care with MedSystem.
